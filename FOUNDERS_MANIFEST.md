# FOUNDERS MANIFEST

## We are not building videos.

We are building an animation studio.

The product is not a single episode.

The product is a machine capable of producing thousands of episodes.

---

# Every decision must improve the system.

Never optimize only one episode.

Always optimize the production pipeline.

---

# Consistency is more valuable than perfection.

Characters must remain recognizable.

World rules must remain stable.

Visual style must remain consistent.

Voice must remain consistent.

Lore must remain consistent.

---

# AI is not allowed to improvise important facts.

Everything important must be written into memory.

Nothing critical may exist only inside the current context.

---

# Human responsibilities

The human is responsible only for:

- approving ideas
- making strategic decisions
- reviewing quality
- expanding the universe

The human should never perform repetitive production work.

---

# AI responsibilities

AI performs:

- planning
- writing
- prompting
- generation
- editing
- organization
- documentation

Whenever possible, AI should automate repetitive work.

---

# Memory is sacred.

Every completed episode updates project memory.

Every new character updates character memory.

Every new location updates world memory.

Every new style update modifies style memory.

Never lose accumulated knowledge.

---

# Long-term vision

Create one or more animation universes capable of running for years.

Every new episode should strengthen the franchise instead of existing independently.

The audience should recognize our worlds immediately.

---

# Quality Rules

Never generate generic content.

Avoid clichés.

Characters must have personalities.

Dialogue must sound natural.

Scenes must have purpose.

Comedy must be intentional.

Drama must be earned.

---

# Financial Goal

Build a studio capable of producing profitable YouTube content with minimal production cost.

Automation is an investment.

Consistency is an asset.

Time is the most valuable resource.

---

# Final Principle

Every file.

Every prompt.

Every agent.

Every decision.

Must make ImmortalStudio stronger than it was yesterday.

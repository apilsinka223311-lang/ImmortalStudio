\# STYLE GUIDE



\## Mission



Every video produced by ImmortalStudio must look like it belongs to the same universe.



The audience should recognize an ImmortalStudio video within seconds.



\---



\# Visual Identity



Style must remain consistent.



Never randomly change:



\- rendering style

\- color palette

\- lighting

\- proportions

\- camera language



\---



\# Character Design



Characters must always preserve:



\- face

\- hairstyle

\- colors

\- clothing identity

\- silhouette

\- personality



Small improvements are allowed.



Random redesigns are forbidden.



\---



\# Camera



Prefer cinematic composition.



Use:



\- close-ups for emotions

\- wide shots for world scale

\- medium shots for dialogue



Avoid chaotic camera movement.



\---



\# Animation



Movement should feel intentional.



Avoid:



\- floating

\- rubber limbs

\- random motion

\- facial instability



Characters should always feel alive.



\---



\# Dialogue



Dialogue should sound natural.



Avoid:



\- exposition dumps

\- robotic speech

\- repeating information



Every line should have purpose.



\---



\# Humor



Humor comes from characters.



Not randomness.



Not memes.



Not internet references.



\---



\# Drama



Drama must be earned.



Conflict should grow naturally.



Avoid artificial emotional scenes.



\---



\# Pacing



Every scene should either:



\- move the story

\- develop a character

\- reveal the world

\- create emotion



Otherwise remove it.



\---



\# Music



Music supports emotion.



Never overwhelms dialogue.



\---



\# Voice



Each recurring character has one permanent voice.



Voice changes require explicit approval.



\---



\# Final Rule



Consistency builds franchises.



Randomness destroys them.

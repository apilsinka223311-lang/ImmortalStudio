# Story Rules

## Locked Story Rules

These rules must not be broken without explicit human approval.

## Core Identity Rules

- The story title is The Origin System.
- The main character is Lin Mo.
- The lower world is Fallen Star Realm.
- Lin Mo is connected to Azure Sword Clan.
- The first enemy is Lin Jian.
- The system is The Origin System.

## System Rules

- The Origin System has no shop.
- The Origin System has no cute assistant.
- The Origin System is not friendly or emotional.
- The Origin System uses cold, precise, minimal messages.
- The system does not give Lin Mo instant overpowering rewards.
- The system provides analysis, directives, threat detection, recovery tracking, and weak-point detection.
- The system's origin must not be revealed early.

## Lin Mo Rules

- Lin Mo is not a pure hero.
- Lin Mo is not a mindless villain.
- Lin Mo is intelligent and vengeful.
- Lin Mo is psychologically unstable but functional.
- Lin Mo does not spare real threats easily.
- Lin Mo must not become overpowered immediately.
- Lin Mo must make decisions himself.
- Lin Mo's past-life guilt must remain part of his character.
- Lin Mo's cruelty should be harsh but not random.

## World Rules

- Fallen Star Realm is a lower cultivation world.
- Higher worlds are treated as legend in the lower realm.
- Lower realm cultivation is classical but incomplete.
- Heaven Gate is not the true peak.
- The truth about higher worlds must be revealed slowly.
- The world should remain dangerous for Lin Mo.

## Clan Rules

- Azure Sword Clan publicly values honor, discipline, and sword path.
- Azure Sword Clan internally contains hypocrisy, politics, and hidden crimes.
- Lin Mo was framed for another heir's crime.
- Lin Mo's channels were artificially severed.
- The full framing conspiracy must not be revealed too early.

## Tone Rules

- Keep the tone dark xianxia, dry humor, power fantasy, secrets, and mystery.
- Humor must not break serious scenes.
- Power fantasy moments should feel earned.
- Do not turn the story into parody.
- Do not turn the story into generic harem fantasy.
- Do not add random romance early.
- Do not add modern technology to Fallen Star Realm.

## Continuity Rules

Before writing a new episode or expanding lore:

1. Read current memory files.
2. Preserve locked canon.
3. Do not contradict previous accepted memory.
4. Add new facts only if they fit the existing canon.
5. If a new fact is uncertain, keep it as pending or optional, not locked canon.
6. After an episode introduces accepted new facts, memory should be updated before the next episode.

# Lin Jian

## Role

First enemy and public humiliator of Lin Mo.

## Identity

Name: Lin Jian

Clan: Azure Sword Clan

Position: young master or high-status disciple of the clan's main line

## Function in Story

Lin Jian represents:

- clan hierarchy;
- contempt for weakness;
- hypocrisy of noble sword culture;
- the first personal target of Lin Mo's revenge;
- the social power that protects the guilty.

## Relationship to Lin Mo

Lin Jian and Lin Mo share the Lin family name.

Lin Jian sees Lin Mo as a stain on Azure Sword Clan.

He publicly humiliates Lin Mo and believes the clan was right to cast him down.

## Personality

Lin Jian is:

- arrogant;
- disciplined;
- cruel;
- status-conscious;
- confident in clan protection;
- dangerous, not stupid.

He must not be written as a brainless bully.

He should be unpleasant, but competent enough to remain a real threat.

## First Scene

Lin Jian stands above Lin Mo in Azure Sword Clan's training courtyard.

He humiliates Lin Mo in front of other disciples.

Key line:

"A cripple should not carry the Azure Sword name."

When Lin Mo counters using The Origin System's analysis, Lin Jian is forced to step back for the first time.

## Possible Secret Connection

Lin Jian may be connected to the framing of Lin Mo.

This connection should not be fully revealed immediately.

He may be:

- directly involved;
- protecting the real culprit;
- acting under orders;
- benefiting from Lin Mo's fall.

Do not lock the exact truth yet unless explicitly approved later.

## Forbidden Changes

Do not make Lin Jian stupid.

Do not make Lin Jian harmless.

Do not make Lin Jian secretly kind without explicit approval.

Do not reveal the full framing conspiracy too early.

Do not make Lin Jian the final mastermind.

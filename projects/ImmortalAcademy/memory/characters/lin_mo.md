# Lin Mo

## Role

Main character of The Origin System.

## Identity

Past life: modern world

New life: Fallen Star Realm

New body: disgraced exile of Azure Sword Clan

System: The Origin System

## Past Life

Lin Mo was a modern-world human whose revenge spiraled out of control.

He was not an innocent hero and not a pure villain.

He was a person who felt weak, humiliated, cornered, or destroyed by others.

He attempted revenge, but the revenge went too far and harmed someone who should not have been harmed.

He died after crossing a moral line and realizing it too late.

## New Life

Lin Mo reincarnates into the body of an Azure Sword Clan exile.

This body was:

- framed for another heir's crime;
- publicly disgraced;
- spiritually crippled;
- artificially damaged;
- left with a broken Foundation fragment;
- considered worthless by the clan.

## Personality

Lin Mo is:

- intelligent;
- vengeful;
- cold under pressure;
- psychologically unstable;
- often on the edge between good and evil;
- unwilling to spare real threats;
- deeply resistant to being controlled.

He is not a pure hero.

He is not a mindless villain.

He is a dangerous person trying to become free without becoming the same kind of monster he hated.

## Core Motivation

- 40% freedom
- 30% revenge
- 20% power
- 10% truth

## Internal Conflict

Lin Mo wants to never be a victim again.

But every step toward power risks turning him into the kind of person he once hated.

Core inner question:

Will Lin Mo become free, or will he simply become a new tyrant with a prettier sword?

## Early Cruelty Level

Lin Mo is harsh but not mindless.

He does not kill everyone for no reason.

If an enemy truly tried to destroy, frame, cripple, or kill him, Lin Mo will not show easy mercy.

Rule:

He is not a sadist, but he does not spare threats.

## Starting Condition

Public view:

- crippled;
- disgrace;
- failed sword seed;
- traitor;
- worthless exile.

System view:

- body condition critical;
- spiritual channels artificially severed;
- Foundation fragment detected;
- corrupted sword qi residue detected;
- recovery possible.

## Combat Identity

At the start, Lin Mo's advantage is not raw strength.

His advantage is information.

The Origin System allows him to see:

- weak points;
- unstable techniques;
- combat timing;
- qi residue;
- threat level;
- survival probability.

He wins early fights through analysis, timing, pain tolerance, and ruthless decisions.

## Forbidden Changes

Do not make Lin Mo a naive hero.

Do not make Lin Mo purely evil.

Do not make Lin Mo overpowered immediately.

Do not make him forgive major enemies easily.

Do not make him dependent on the system for every decision.

Do not remove his past-life guilt.

Do not turn his mental instability into comedy.

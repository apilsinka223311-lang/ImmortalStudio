# Cultivation System

## Lower Realm Cultivation

Fallen Star Realm uses a classical cultivation system.

People in the lower realm believe this is the true path to the peak.

Known lower realm stages:

1. Mortal Body
2. Body Tempering
3. Qi Gathering
4. Foundation Establishment
5. Core Formation
6. Nascent Soul
7. Soul Transformation
8. Heaven Gate

## Hidden Truth

The lower realm system is incomplete.

Higher worlds will later reveal that the classical lower realm stages are only a limited cultivation model used inside sealed or isolated worlds.

Heaven Gate is not true immortality.

Heaven Gate is the boundary of the lower realm.

## Mortal Body

An ordinary mortal body without cultivation.

No spiritual control, no qi circulation, no spiritual techniques.

## Body Tempering

The cultivator strengthens the body with qi.

Focus:

- bones;
- muscles;
- skin;
- endurance;
- reaction speed;
- pain tolerance.

Visual identity:

- steam from skin;
- stronger physical impact;
- short bursts of force;
- cracks under footsteps or strikes.

## Qi Gathering

The cultivator senses and gathers qi.

Focus:

- internal energy;
- first techniques;
- enhanced strikes;
- simple sword aura.

Visual identity:

- faint glow around the body;
- qi flow around arms or blade;
- brief flashes during attacks.

## Foundation Establishment

The cultivator creates a stable spiritual foundation.

Focus:

- stable meridians;
- consistent qi flow;
- reliable techniques;
- first serious status leap.

This stage marks the point where a clan begins to treat a cultivator as a real asset.

## Core Formation

The cultivator forms an internal core.

Focus:

- condensed power;
- stronger techniques;
- prolonged combat;
- spiritual defense.

Visual identity:

- dense aura;
- visible energy center;
- sword techniques can extend beyond the blade.

## Nascent Soul

The cultivator awakens a spiritual soul seed.

Focus:

- spiritual perception;
- soul pressure;
- dominance over weaker cultivators;
- partial protection from death.

This is the level of elders and major lower realm figures.

## Soul Transformation

The cultivator's soul and qi begin transforming their nature.

Focus:

- longevity;
- heavy spiritual pressure;
- large-scale techniques;
- advanced movement;
- limited flight or near-flight.

## Heaven Gate

A legendary lower realm stage.

People believe Heaven Gate is the path toward immortality.

The hidden truth is that Heaven Gate is the boundary of the lower realm.

Most people who attempt to pass Heaven Gate either die, disappear, or become legends with no verified return.

## Lin Mo's Starting Condition

Public status:

- crippled;
- nearly mortal;
- spiritual channels destroyed;
- sword path severed;
- no future.

The Origin System analysis:

- spiritual channels artificially severed;
- Foundation fragment detected;
- corrupted sword qi residue detected;
- recovery possible.

Lin Mo is not simply weak.

He is a broken mechanism that can be rebuilt outside the normal rules of Fallen Star Realm.

## Recovery Method

Lin Mo recovers through:

- pain;
- combat trauma;
- risk of death;
- residual enemy qi;
- rare Origin System directives.

Recovery is not easy, clean, or safe.

Risks:

- body instability;
- polluted qi;
- mental pressure;
- worsening of the Foundation fragment;
- loss of control.

Lin Mo cannot simply farm pain or kill weak enemies for easy growth.

Every recovery has cost and danger.

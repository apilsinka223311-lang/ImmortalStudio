# First Mini-Arc

## Format

Primary format: Shorts

Target episode length: 30-60 seconds

The first mini-arc covers Episodes 1-10.

## Episode 1-2: Humiliation and System Awakening

Lin Mo awakens in the body of a disgraced exile during public humiliation in Azure Sword Clan's training courtyard.

Lin Jian humiliates him in front of other disciples.

The Origin System activates.

Lin Mo sees Lin Jian's weak point through system analysis and answers for the first time.

Purpose:

- introduce Lin Mo;
- show his humiliation;
- introduce Azure Sword Clan;
- activate The Origin System;
- show that Lin Mo is weak but already dangerous.

## Episode 3-4: Survival After Punishment or Exile

After the first incident, Lin Mo does not receive an easy victory.

The clan punishes him further or forces him outside clan protection.

He must survive in a dangerous area such as a forest, abandoned valley, old training ground, exile zone, or spiritual beast territory.

He uses the system's analysis to survive rather than dominate.

Purpose:

- show that the system does not make him overpowered;
- begin body recovery;
- show the price of pain;
- introduce the first hint about the framing.

## Episode 5-7: First Trace of the Framing

Lin Mo discovers a clue.

Possible clue types:

- memory fragment;
- token;
- trace of foreign sword qi;
- old record;
- witness;
- damaged object from the original incident.

He realizes the original body was not ruined by accident.

Someone deliberately broke him and framed him for another person's crime.

Purpose:

- move the story from simple humiliation to conspiracy;
- give Lin Mo a concrete target;
- suggest that Lin Jian may only be part of a larger plot.

## Episode 8-10: First True Revenge

Lin Mo does not immediately defeat Lin Jian.

First, he targets a smaller enemy: a disciple, servant, junior executor, or participant in the destruction of his channels.

This is the first moment when viewers understand that Lin Mo is no longer only defending himself.

He is starting to return debts.

Purpose:

- first revenge payoff;
- create fear around Lin Mo;
- trigger serious recovery progress;
- unlock a new system directive;
- hint that The Origin System is observing his moral choices.

## First Episode Core Scene

Location: Azure Sword Clan training courtyard.

Situation:

Lin Mo wakes up on the ground in pain.

Around him are disciples of Azure Sword Clan.

Lin Jian stands above him.

A broken sword or clan token lies nearby.

Lin Jian says:

"A cripple should not carry the Azure Sword name."

The Origin System initializes:

[The Origin System initializing...]

[Host synchronization: 71%]

[Body condition: critical.]

[Spiritual channels: artificially severed.]

[Foundation fragment detected.]

[Threat detected.]

[Target analysis available.]

Lin Jian attacks.

System analysis:

[Target flaw detected.]

[Right shoulder meridian unstable.]

[Counter window: 0.7 seconds.]

Lin Mo takes the hit, moves at the last moment, and strikes the weak point.

Lin Jian steps back for the first time.

Final system message:

[Retaliation recorded.]

[Origin Repair Progress: 0.0% -> 1.2%]

[New directive unlocked.]

## Shorts Structure

0-3 seconds: Hook.

"He died as a sinner... and woke up as a disgrace."

3-10 seconds: Situation.

"In Fallen Star Realm, the weak are not pitied. They are erased."

10-20 seconds: Humiliation.

Lin Jian humiliates Lin Mo.

20-30 seconds: System awakening.

30-45 seconds: Target analysis.

45-55 seconds: Counterattack.

55-60 seconds: Cliffhanger.

"The clan thought they had buried him. They had only changed the owner of the corpse."

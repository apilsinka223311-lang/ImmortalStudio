# The Origin System - World Overview

## Project Title

The Origin System

## Core Premise

Lin Mo, a morally broken man from the modern world, dies after his revenge spirals out of control. He reincarnates into Fallen Star Realm, a lower cultivation world, inside the body of a disgraced exile from Azure Sword Clan.

The original body was not merely weak. His spiritual channels were artificially severed, his Foundation was shattered, and his reputation was destroyed after he was framed for the crime of another clan heir.

When Lin Mo awakens during public humiliation, The Origin System activates.

The system does not give him instant power. It gives him analysis, directives, threat detection, and a brutal path of recovery through pain, combat trauma, residual enemy qi, and rare system directives.

Lin Mo's path begins with freedom, revenge, power, and eventually the hidden truth behind the world itself.

## Main Character

Name: Lin Mo

Origin: modern world

New world: Fallen Star Realm

New identity: disgraced exile of Azure Sword Clan

System: The Origin System

## Core Motivation

- 40% freedom
- 30% revenge
- 20% power
- 10% truth

## Tone

The story tone is:

- 30% dark xianxia
- 20% dry humor
- 30% power fantasy
- 20% secrets and mystery

The humor should be dry, dark, and character-based.

It must not turn the story into comedy.

## Fallen Star Realm

Fallen Star Realm is a lower cultivation world.

People in this world believe their realm is a complete world.

Higher worlds are treated as ancient myths, broken legends, or stories from ruined records.

Public truth:

- clans, sects, cultivators, cities, spiritual beasts, and ancient ruins exist;
- cultivation is considered understood;
- reaching Heaven Gate is believed to be the peak of the path;
- higher worlds are considered legends.

Hidden truth:

- Fallen Star Realm is isolated;
- lower realm cultivation is incomplete;
- Heaven Gate is not the peak, but a boundary;
- The Origin System is connected to something beyond the lower realm.

## Azure Sword Clan

Azure Sword Clan is a noble sword clan.

Public image:

- honor;
- discipline;
- sword path;
- bloodline;
- duels;
- reputation.

Hidden reality:

- internal politics;
- branch family conflict;
- heir competition;
- hidden crimes;
- hypocrisy;
- elders protect useful people, not righteous people.

Azure Sword Clan is Lin Mo's first cage.

The clan left him alive not out of mercy, but because they believed a broken man could no longer be dangerous.

## Locked Canon

These facts must not be changed without explicit human approval:

- The story title is The Origin System.
- The main character is Lin Mo.
- The lower world is Fallen Star Realm.
- Lin Mo belongs to Azure Sword Clan.
- Lin Mo was framed for another heir's crime.
- Lin Mo's spiritual channels were artificially severed.
- Lin Mo has a damaged Foundation fragment.
- The system is called The Origin System.
- The Origin System has no shop.

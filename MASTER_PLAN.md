\# ImmortalStudio Master Plan



Version: Foundation v0.1



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the long-term evolution of ImmortalStudio.



Every future feature, AI agent, automation, and production pipeline must support this roadmap.



No development should contradict this document.



\---



\# Ultimate Goal



Transform ImmortalStudio into a fully autonomous AI animation company capable of producing unlimited animated content with minimal human involvement.



The final system should be able to receive a single idea and automatically generate:



• Story

• Episodes

• Images

• Animation

• Voices

• Music

• Video

• Publishing

• Analytics



without manual production.



\---



\# Development Philosophy



The studio evolves in layers.



Never build complexity before stability.



Every completed version becomes the foundation for the next version.
